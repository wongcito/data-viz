Good Visualizations

Billetón Electoral [Ojo Publico]

Design Choices
  • By using interactivity, users are able to input how much they earn to see a comparison with how much each presidential candidate earns.
  • There is also a bar chart comparing each candidate.
Why it works
  • It was released during the presidential election in Peru so it gather a lot of attention.
  • It was easy to use and share in social networks.
Goal and potential improvements
  • Show how much each presidential candidate earned and compare it with anyone.
  • Introduce data visualization and newsapps to the Peruvian audience.
  • The visualization could have had a better design or even include a simple newsgame.

Zika Spread [Miami Herald]

Design Choices
  • The article uses data visualization to show the state of infection of the Zika virus in Miami.
  • It combines storytelling with interactive charts.
Why it works
  • Usually, these projects are done with non-current events, however this chart showed that these tools can also be useful in things that people care.
  • Adding a storytelling layer gives readers more reasons to continue reading.
Goal and potential improvements
  • The goal was to introduce a visualization quickly, so it's useful to people.
  • It could be improved by adding a search bar for people to introduce their ZIP code and know the state in their vecinity.

Bad Visualizations

Medal Leaders [Columbus Dispatch]

Design Choices
  • Use of bars to compare Olympic medals for different contries.
  • They wanted to create an easy to read chart.
Why it works (or not)
  • It's difficult to see the differences between each bar (8 > 9?)
  • This type of bar chart it's not the best option to show this data.
Goal and potential improvements
  • Their goal was to compare three countries in the Olympics.
  • It could be improved by using a series vertical bar chart.

Domestic Oil Production [British Petroleum]

Design Choices
  • They tried to use a design that resembles to oil extraction.
  • They wanted to make an easy to read chart by not introducing numbers or scales.
Why it works (or not)
  • It doesn't use a scale, so we don't know of what are we talking about.
  • It doesn't show any data, it doesn't seem reliable.
Goal and potential improvements
  • The goal was to show how the US is the biggest oil producer in the World.
  • It could be improved by adding a scale and better tags.
